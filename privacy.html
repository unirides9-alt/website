Privacy Policy

This page explains how unirides collects, uses, and protects information when you use the student cab booking app and website.

Information we collect

We collect basic account details (such as name, student email, and phone number), ride information (pickup, drop, time, fare), and technical data needed to secure your account and improve the service.

How we use your information

We use your information to create and manage your account, match you with drivers, send transactional emails (like OTPs and ride receipts), provide support, and improve safety and reliability.

Emails and notifications

unirides sends transactional messages only, such as OTP codes, registration confirmations, ride receipts, and important security alerts. We do not send promotional or marketing newsletters.

Data sharing

Limited information is shared with drivers and service providers as needed to complete your trips and operate our platform. We do not sell your personal information.

Security

We use industry‑standard measures to protect your data in transit and at rest, including TLS encryption and secure infrastructure. You should keep your device and login methods secure as well.

Your choices

You can request access, update, or deletion of your account data by contacting us at unirides9@gmail.com. Some data may be retained where required by law or for fraud prevention.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at unirides9@gmail.com.
